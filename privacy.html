Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

Only your TikTok OAuth token (stored locally on your device) and voice samples you voluntarily upload.

OAuth token used only to publish videos to your TikTok. Voice samples processed locally for narration only.

3. Storage

All data stored locally on your device in ~/HorrorAI/. Nothing stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Uses Groq, Pexels, Pixabay, TikTok, Chatterbox TTS APIs — each with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Video URLs sent to TikTok only when you explicitly publish.

6. Delete Your Data

Delete ~/HorrorAI/ folder to remove all local data.
